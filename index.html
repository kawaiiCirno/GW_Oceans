Global Warming

And the Oceans


The effects and consequences of the warming of the oceans.


Effects

Sea Level Risemore_vert

IPCC

Sea Level Riseclose
Impacts

As the global temperature increases due to global warming, the average ocean temperature also increases. Therefore, from the thermal expansion of the water and the gradual melting of ice sheets, the average sea level increases. Since the Industrial Revolution, the sea level rose ~8 inches

Effects

Coastal cities will be in greater threat for flooding, destroying buildings and disrupting natural habitats.

Extreme Weathermore_vert

IPCC

Extreme Weatherclose
Cyclones

With the average increase in global temperature, the temperature of surface water will increase, meaning that there is more energy stored in the water. As hurricanes and tornadoes draw their energy in the form of heat, this will generally mean stronger hurricanes and tornadoes.

Unpredictiality

With unknown disturbances in ocean and atmospheric circulation, this would mean in general more unpredictable and chaotic weather.





Ocean Acificationmore_vert

Wikipedia

Ocean Acificationclose
Cause

With the increased in carbon dioxide in the atmosphere from human emissions, more of it will react with the ocean. Although minimizing the CO2 concentration from the atmosphere, the biproduct of storing carbon in the water is the increased acidity of the water.

Effects

Many organisms will be affected by the change in pH, dirsupting oceanic ecosystems.

Further Warmingmore_vert

Paper

The Sulfur Cycleclose

Sulfur fluxes from the ocean help keep the temperatuer cool by promoting cloud condensation, with phytoplakton being the main source of it. As phytoplankton are extremely sensetive to pH, the amount of sulfur released will decrease, creating a positive feedback loop between the ocean and carbon fluxes.

Things You Can Do

  • Self Changes
    Lessen the amount of carbon emissions used individually! Use less energy and use energy efficient products. Power your home with renewable energy sources.
  • Politics
    Worldwide, large-scale change is needed to make a impact on climate change. Vote for politians in favor for clean energy policies. Email or call your local representative to ask about their climate change stances.
  • Spread the Word
    Inform others on the dangers of global warming and climate change, and ways to combat it.
